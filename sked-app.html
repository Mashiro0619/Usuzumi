功能 Features

不是再做一个日历，而是减少安排本身的摩擦。 Not another calendar — less friction around planning itself.

Sked 的重点不是把所有事情塞进格子里，而是帮你看清时间、精力和优先级之间的关系。 Sked is not about fitting everything into boxes. It helps you see the relationship between time, energy, and priority.

日程块 Time blocks

用时间块组织一天，把会议、专注、零碎任务分开，不让清单吞掉日历。 Organize the day with time blocks, separating meetings, focus work, and small tasks.

轻量复盘 Lightweight review

每天结束时记录完成度、延期原因和明天最重要的一件事。 At the end of the day, record progress, delays, and the one thing that matters tomorrow.

安静提醒 Quiet alerts

提醒只在必要时出现，用低干扰的方式提示下一段安排。 Notifications appear only when needed, quietly guiding you to the next block.

界面 Screens

三个核心界面，覆盖从计划到复盘的完整流程。 Three core screens cover the full flow from planning to review.

Today

当天时间块、任务与下一步行动。 Today’s blocks, tasks, and next action.

Plan

快速拖拽任务到可用时间段。 Quickly drag tasks into available time slots.

Review

查看完成度与明日优先级。 Review progress and tomorrow’s priority.
